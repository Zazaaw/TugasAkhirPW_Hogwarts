Project Description :
//
This project aims to create a simple school website that will serve various stakeholders, including students, parents, teachers, and all those involved in the field of education. With a focus on providing easy access to school news and related information, the website will become the primary information hub for all things related to the school. Through the development of this website, we aim to ensure that all stakeholders have easily accessible resources to follow school developments and obtain relevant information.
